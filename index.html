About Me

I'm a New York City native focused on graphic and web design, UI/UX design, and photography (I code a bit too.) As an aficionado of simple pleasures, I find that music, coffee, great pizza, dogs, and a view of the ocean enhance my creativity.

I enjoy solving visual problems to foster communication and ease of use, and challenge myself to create beauty in each design.

During my career I’ve had the opportunity to work in various industries, from corporate, financial, and legal, to higher education and nonprofit, with some design/marketing firms interspersed.

I’m currently pursuing new opportunities as a graphic and UI designer.
